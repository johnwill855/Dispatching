About Company

Creating Long-Term Value For Stakeholder.

Profleet Logistics was founded with a bold vision—to revolutionize the truck dispatching industry by addressing the inefficiencies and delays plaguing logistics. Starting from humble beginnings, our founders overcame challenges such as market competition, limited resources, and evolving technology by staying committed to innovation and quality service. Today, Profleet Logictics stands as a trusted name, marked by major milestones like expanding our dispatch network nationwide, integrating cutting-edge tracking systems, and consistently exceeding customer expectations.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide unparalleled dispatching services, delivering reliability, efficiency, and complete customer satisfaction. We strive to optimize every mile, every delivery, every time.

Our Services

Efficient and Reliable Dispatch Services

Timely Logistix offers a comprehensive range of truck dispatching services tailored to help carriers optimize their routes, maximize profits, and reduce operational stress. We are committed to delivering top-tier dispatching solutions that ensure every mile counts.

Full Truckload (FTL)

We coordinate and manage entire truckloads to ensure timely pickups, optimized routes, and fast, efficient deliveries.

Less Than Truckload (LTL)

We efficiently handle LTL shipments by combining loads, optimizing routes, and minimizing carrier costs.

Refrigerated (Reefer)

We specialize in dispatching temperature-sensitive loads, ensuring goods arrive fresh and in perfect condition.

Flatbed Dispatching

We manage specialized equipment and oversized loads with safety and compliance at the forefront.

Dedicated Dispatchers

Our dedicated dispatchers offer personalized service, focusing on your unique needs and business goals.

How It Works

From onboarding to delivery, we handle load matching, real-time updates, and complete load management seamlessly.

Technology & Tools

We leverage state-of-the-art software, load tracking, and 24/7 tech-enabled support for smooth operations.
